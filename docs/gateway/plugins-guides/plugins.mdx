This page lists plugins for seamless integration between Fondy and popular CMS and e-commerce platforms. These plugins allow you to connect Fondy's payment gateway to your website, enabling smooth payment processing for your online store or content management system. Whether you're using a CMS like WordPress or Joomla or an e-commerce platform like Magento or Shopify, we've got you covered with ready-made solutions for your platform.
