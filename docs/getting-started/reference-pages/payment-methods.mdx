Fondy provides a comprehensive range of payment options to accommodate diverse customer needs. By offering global and local payment methods, Fondy simplifies the payment process, helping businesses increase conversion rates and deliver a seamless checkout experience.

This page outlines the supported payment methods and their key attributes, including:

* **Payment Method Category**: Classifies the type of payment method (e.g., card payments, bank transfers, wallets).
* **Supported Countries for Payers**: Indicates the countries where payers can use the method.
* **Countries of Business Registration**: Specifies where a business must be registered to enable the payment method.
